Standard shipping within Metro Manila takes 2-3 business days. Provincial shipping takes 3-5 business days. Express shipping (additional fee) delivers within 24 hours for Metro Manila orders.

We accept GCash, PayMaya, credit/debit cards, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for Philippine orders. International orders require credit/debit card payment.

We offer a 30-day return policy for unopened, unused products. Opened products can be returned within 7 days if defective. Please contact our support team to initiate a return.
